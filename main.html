About Us

Why AMK Cosmetics?

We created AMK Cosmetics to give our fellow makeup enthusiasts the access to the products that we love and use every single day to create awesome looks. Whether getting your makeup done with a glam team for a shoot, or doing your own makeup at home, we love that makeup can make us feel confident and beautiful and we want to share that feeling with you guys.

Our new, updated line features all of our favorite iconic Lip Kit shades as well as my must-haves including blushes, bronzers, highlighters, high glosses, matte liquid lipsticks, lip blushes, brow essentials and eyeliners.
